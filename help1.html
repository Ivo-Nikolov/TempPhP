SPECIAL SYMBOLS AND FEATURES

Free Spins

Three Free Spins symbols anywhere on the reels trigger the Free Spins Bonus. All bonus wins are doubled and you can win more free spins in the free spins bonus.

2xWild

The Wild symbol substitutes for all paying symbols and doubles all wins.

Dragon

The Dragon can turn up to 3 reels into Wild symbols for Big Wins!

HOW TO PLAY CHINESE TREASURES

Total Stake

The Total Stake is the total of stakes per line. You can adjust it at any time, using the + or - buttons.

Spin the Reels

If you are playing on desktop, click the SPIN button or press the spacebar on your keyboard to spin the reels. Speed up each reel spin by pressing the spacebar again, or by clicking on the reels.

If you are playing on a mobile device, tap the SPIN button or drag the reels to spin. Speed up each reel spin by tapping the same button or the reels again.

Autoplay

Press the AUTO button to commence automatic play of the game at the currently selected stake. Select how many spins to play via the auto play control box. Stop auto play by pressing the STOP button.
